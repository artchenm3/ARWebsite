About Ariex

The Ariex Story

Established in 2008, Ariex has been crafting high quality table tennis equipment for over 30 years as a manufacturer for well-known European, Japanese and Chinese brands. We source top quality materials from Japan, and handle the design and manufacturing in our state of the art facility in Taiwan. We strongly believe racket design is an art form, and hold ourselves to the highest standard for the quality of our products.

The Hinoki Difference

Kiso-Hinoki Racket

Hinoki, or Japanese Cypress, is a high quality timber known for its rich, straight wood grain pattern, natural scent of fragrance and high resilience to decay. Due to those qualities, it’s often used to build traditional Japanese temples. Hinoki trees in the Kiso Mountain are especially known for their quality, and nicknamed Go-Shin-Boku, “Trees where God stayed.” Kiso Hinoki trees are under strict national conservation protection and only a small quantity can be harvested annually.


Hinoki wood is considered top material for table tennis racket, with its lightness, soft feel and power. Players who have experienced playing with a mono-ply Hinoki racket often comment on how connected they feel with each hitting of the ball and the amount of control they have for the shot. Ariex sources the Kiso Hinoki woods directly from Japan, and with its high quality engineering process, turning it into a piece of art that will be appreciated by all players.
